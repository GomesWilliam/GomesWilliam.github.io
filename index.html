Intro

Hello everybody, William here. I am a guy from Brazil and I was born in 1997 in São Paulo, SP. I am currently studying engineering and science (bachelor degree) and there’s a lot of things about me that I would like to share with you all is related to these topics.

My interest in technology sprung up in my early age when I used to play a lot of games in my father's computer and I also used to watch him fixing electronics equipments. I believe that my personal interest in technology and my math easeness during elementary school carried me out to the world of science and I was determined to study engineering. Surprisingly, it was not electrical engineering or computer science my first choice when I was high school wondering about my university, but in Civil engineering. It turns out that my desire by electrical engineering and Computer Science came after high school, and here I am a Information Engineering student at UFABC, which was a great choice that makes me really happy.

I deeply believe that education has the power to change the world, if every single person carry out their role of pursuing knowledge. As a little piece of this enormous puzzle, I really hope to help people by spreading knowledge and new ideas, supporting organizations and contributing to create a better world.
